- These gestures are evaluator-friendly general gestures only. They are not ASL medical predictions.

2. How Wave is detected
- The component stores a rolling buffer of the last 30 recognition frames.
- Wave requires Open_Palm and repeated left-right horizontal motion of wrist/index-tip position.
- Tiny jitter is ignored using a minimum per-frame movement threshold.
- A hand must show meaningful horizontal range and at least two direction changes before Wave is proposed.
- The final label is debounced and considered stable after about 650 ms.

3. How swipe gestures are detected
- Swipe_Left and Swipe_Right are detected from clear horizontal movement over recent frames.
- Horizontal movement must exceed the swipe threshold and be stronger than vertical movement.
- This logic runs only in the local browser rule engine.

4. How dual-hand gestures are detected
- Two_Hands_Open requires two detected hands with Open_Palm labels.
- Double_Thumbs_Up requires two detected hands with Thumb_Up labels.
- Double_Fist requires two detected hands with Closed_Fist labels.
- Two_Hand_Wave requires two hands, at least Open_Palm involvement, and wave motion on both hands.
